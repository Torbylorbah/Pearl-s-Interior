About Us

Pearl's was established in February 2019. The firm provides high-quality, furnished interiors for residential and commercial projects. Commercial work, including retail, hospitality and office interior design are provided in collaboration with licensed architectural and engineering partners. Using AutoCAD and Autodesk 3D StudioMax, the firm also offers in-house professional design documents, renderings and virtual walk-through animations for its clients.
